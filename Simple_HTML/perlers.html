Welcome!

I'm just someone who likes making things out of perler beads.

Some perlers I made include:

  • Haikyuu!! jerseys
  • Smash balls
  • Pokemon game sprites

How to make Perlers:

  1. Gather the beads you need.
  2. Put it on a pegboard.
  3. Iron it.
  4. Wait for it to cool.
  5. Done!
